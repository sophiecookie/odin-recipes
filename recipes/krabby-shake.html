Krabby Shake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberry ice cream
  • 1 cup vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • Whipped cream, for garnish
  • Fresh whole cherries, for garnish

Steps

  1. To begin, add your strawberry and vanilla ice cream scoops inside a blender or food processor.
  2. Then, add the whole milk inside too. Only start with 3/4 cup of milk.
  3. Blend the ingredients together until you get a uniform texture. It only takes about 20-30 seconds.
  4. If you want to adjust the consistency of the milkshake, add a little more milk. Blend the ingredients again.
  5. Once you are happy with the consistency, pour the SpongeBob strawberry milkshake into milkshake glasses.
  6. Top each milkshake with a big dollop of whipped cream and a delicious fresh cherry, just like SpongeBob did!
